
# Research Overview

My research focuses on star formation.
In recent years, I have been working particularly on the formation of star clusters.
I also develop numerical simulation codes for radiation transfer and hydrodynamics.


# Keywords
- Star cluster formation
- Radiation hydrodynamics simulations
- Massive star formation
- Circularly polarized light generated by dust grains
